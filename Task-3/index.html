Features

Everything your team needs

From planning to deployment — all in one place, with zero friction.

Unified Workspace
Bring your tasks, docs, and code reviews into a single, distraction-free workspace your whole team can navigate.
Real-time Analytics
Track velocity, deployment frequency, and team health with live dashboards that update the moment things change.
Team Collaboration
Inline comments, review flows, and @mentions keep everyone aligned — no more lost Slack threads or stale email chains.
One-click Deploys
Connect your Git repo and push to production with a single click. Automatic rollbacks keep you safe if anything goes wrong.
Enterprise Security
SOC 2 Type II, SSO, and fine-grained role permissions out of the box. Your data stays yours, always encrypted at rest.
AI Automation
Let Fluxr's AI auto-assign tasks, detect blockers, and suggest next steps so your team focuses on what matters most.
